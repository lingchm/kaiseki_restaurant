Located in the center of Granollers (Barcelona), the WAGAYA restaurant offers typical dishes from Japanese Cuisine and from other countries, fusing flavors and aromas to create new sensations
on the palate.


PreviousNext

Wagaya restaurant opened its doors in 2016. From the beginning, we sought a balance between the best product, a cozy atmosphere and good service, achieving an optimal quality/price ratio. The three main spaces, the "Mar - Bosque - Sol Naciente" rooms, are decorated in a modern style where colors and different ornamental elements create three totally different environments that will allow you to live an always different visual experience while enjoying your favorite dishes.
